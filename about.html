About RV University

RV University is a leading private university located in Bangalore, India. Founded in 1994, the university offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various fields. RV University is known for its academic excellence, strong industry partnerships, and vibrant campus life.

Our Mission

RV Times is an online student-run news magazine dedicated to providing the RV University community with informative and engaging articles on various topics. We strive to be a reliable and credible source of news, while also offering a platform for students to express their voices and creativity.

  • Empower students by providing a platform to develop their skills and share their perspectives.
  • Inform the community by providing accurate and unbiased reporting with insightful analysis.
  • Connect the community through storytelling and sharing diverse viewpoints.

Forget Fake News - We're All About the Fact Check Fiesta!

Tired of misinformation masquerading as reality? Join the party here at RV Times, where we dissect lies like ninjas with truth-seeking swords (metaphorically speaking, of course). We're the student-run news platform that shines a spotlight on campus issues, debunks rumors faster than a squirrel on Red Bull, and keeps you informed with a sprinkle of sass (because who wants boring news, anyway?).

Think the internet is a minefield of misinformation? We're your map, your compass, and your trusty fact-checking sidekick. We delve deep into stories, interview real people (not talking pigeons, though some sources are questionable), and present the truth with a twist of humor and a dash of student perspective. No stuffy jargon, just straight-up facts delivered with enough punch to knock out a conspiracy theory.
